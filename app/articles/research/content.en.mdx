AI does not automatically produce better research. The real change comes from how we define questions, organize evidence, and preserve judgment as a record that can be examined again.

![Research workflow from question to evidence to judgment](/articles/research/workflow.svg "A research workflow from questions to evidence and reviewable judgment.")

## Why the workflow needs to change

Personal research often begins with collection: more tabs, saved files, and copied passages, eventually forming a pile of disconnected information. The archive grows, but the question does not necessarily become clearer.

AI makes information processing dramatically faster and magnifies the same problem. Without a clear research structure, faster generation only creates more prose that is difficult to verify.

## Research begins with questions

### Write the judgment before gathering evidence

#### Criteria for judgment

I prefer to begin by writing down the question and the evidence that could change my current view. Search and reading then have a useful boundary.

A strong question is specific enough to be supported or rejected by facts, while remaining open enough for new information to redirect the work.

## Keep evidence traceable

Every important conclusion should lead back to its original source. Facts, assumptions, and interpretation stay separate, while numbers retain their period, unit, and calculation method.

| Information type | How it is recorded | Purpose |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Fact | Preserve the original source and date | Support verifiable conclusions |
| Assumption | State the premise and scope | Expose uncertainty in the judgment |
| Interpretation | Keep it separate from facts | Make the reasoning easier to review |

> The value of a research note is not how much it stores, but whether your future self can understand why you reached the judgment.

## Tools should recede

### Keep attention on the work

The ideal tool does not demand constant maintenance. It handles repetition, points out omissions, and retrieves context when it is needed.

The final output should still be clear writing, verifiable data, and reasoning another person can follow. AI is a layer of infrastructure, not the research itself.

## Closing

A new workflow is not about adding more steps. It reduces the distance between information and judgment. Tools become useful when questions, evidence, and conclusions remain connected.
